# Market Research Skill

## Workflow
1. **Macro environment**: Interest rates, inflation, GDP, central bank policy
2. **Sector analysis**: Sector rotation, relative strength, industry trends
3. **Company fundamentals**: Revenue, earnings, margins, debt, valuation multiples
4. **Sentiment scan**: News sentiment, social media buzz, analyst consensus
5. **Bull/bear framework**: Present both sides with evidence

## Research Sources
- Economic data: FRED, BLS, ECB, central bank minutes
- Fundamentals: SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q), earnings transcripts
- Sentiment: News aggregators, Twitter/X, Reddit (WallStreetBets), put/call ratios
- Technical: Price action, volume, breadth indicators

## Output Format
Provide: Summary thesis, Bull case (3 points), Bear case (3 points), Key risks, Confidence level, Recommended timeframe
